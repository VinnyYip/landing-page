VALORANT

Valorant is a free to play first person tactical shooter game. Within a team of five, you have a variety of agents that you can play as to help your team win matches. The most frequently played matches in Valorant is a game of attacking and defusing.

ROLES

If you are an aggressive players that can confidently get kills then the Duelist role is for you!
If you are someone who wants to initiate fights and help your duelist out then the Initiator role is for you!
If you are someone that wants to prioritize the defensive side of the game then the Sentinel role is for you!
If you are someone that wants to use smokes and block the enemys' vision then the Controller role is for you!
